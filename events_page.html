Weekly Events

Music in the Park

Enjoying live music in the park is a perfect way to relax and take in the outdoors. Bring a cozy blanket or chairs for comfort, along with snacks like cheese and fruit and your favorite drinks to stay refreshed. If the event goes into the evening, pack a light sweater and maybe some fun lights to enhance the atmosphere. With good company, great music, and the right setup, a park concert becomes a memorable experience for everyone. Happening every Fri/Sat Night, May - September.

Public Pool - Spring/Summer

With Spring and Summer around the corner, swimming at the Bishop pool is a fun and refreshing way to cool off on a hot day. Bring your swimsuit, towel, and sunscreen to stay comfortable while enjoying the water. Whether you're swimming laps, playing with friends, or just lounging poolside, there’s something for everyone. With lifeguards on duty and a lively atmosphere, the Bishop pool is a great spot for summer fun and relaxation. Open everyday, from 8a.m. - 8p.m.

Sunday Evening Adult Soccer Pickup Games

Join us every Sunday night for our adult soccer pickup games, a fun and competitive way to stay active. Whether you're a seasoned player or just looking for a casual game, all skill levels are welcome. Games are organized on the spot, so just bring your cleats, plenty of water, a positive attitude, and be ready to play. Come enjoy great soccer, meet new people, and kick off the week with some friendly competition!

Saturday Morning Farmers Markets

The farmers market is a lively gathering where local vendors offer fresh produce, handmade goods, and delicious treats. It’s a great place to shop for seasonal fruits and vegetables, artisan bread, honey, and unique crafts while supporting small businesses. With live music, food trucks, and family-friendly activities, the market is more than just shopping—it’s a community experience. Bring your reusable bags, explore the stands, and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of fresh food and friendly faces!
